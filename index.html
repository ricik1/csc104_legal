Patricia Wells

C.E.S.M.T 1994

&

HTA level II since 2011

Patricia Wells is a dedicated and skilled Certified Equine Massage Therapist with a profound passion for horses and their well-being. With over a decade of experience working closely with these magnificent animals.

Patricia has honed her expertise in equine massage therapy to provide the highest level of care and comfort to her equine clients. Patricia's journey into the world of equine massage began as a lifelong horse enthusiast.
She recognized the vital role that massage plays in enhancing a horse's physical health and performance.
This realization led her to pursue formal training and certification in equine massage therapy, where she gained a deep understanding of equine anatomy and physiology.

As a therapist, Patricia specializes in tailoring massage sessions to meet the unique needs of each horse. Whether it's relieving muscle tension, aiding in recovery
after intense training, or simply offering relaxation and stress relief, Patricia's gentle touch and intuitive understanding of horses make her a trusted partner for equine owners and trainers.

Beyond her technical skills, Patricia's unwavering commitment to the well-being of her equine clients shines through in her compassionate approach.
She believes that a strong bond of trust between therapist and horse is crucial for successful outcomes.
Patricia takes the time to build that trust, ensuring that each horse feels comfortable and relaxed during their sessions.

Her mission is to contribute to the overall health and happiness of every horse she works with, enabling them to thrive and excel in their respective disciplines. Her commitment to the well-being of horses goes beyond her professional role, making her a respected figure in the equestrian community.

Patricia Wells is not just an equine massage therapist; she's a trusted partner in the journey to keeping horses happy, healthy, and performing at their best.
